Frequently Asked Questions

Is this ATS-friendly?

Yes, all our resume templates are designed to be ATS (Applicant Tracking System) friendly. They pass through automated screening systems used by most companies.

How long does delivery take?

Delivery times vary by package: Starter (2-3 days), Pro (5-7 days), and Premium (7-10 days). We also offer rush delivery for urgent requirements.

Can I edit my resume later?

Absolutely! We provide the source files and instructions on how to edit your resume. Our support team is also available to help with updates.

What if I'm not satisfied with the result?

We offer unlimited revisions until you're completely satisfied. If you're still not happy, we provide a full refund within 7 days of delivery.
